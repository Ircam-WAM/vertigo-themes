Dear starts.eu members,

We contact you to inform you that the terms and conditions of the use of starts.eu will change in the next days.

This change corresponds to the end of the STARTS ECOSYSTEM project, which has been funded for the last 30 months to host, maintain, and animate the starts.eu platform. From December 2021, it will be transferred to a website hosted by Ars Electronica.

The new terms and conditions will follow the policy in place at Ars Electronica, that you can find here: https://ars.electronica.art/about/en/datenschutz/

The good news is: starts.eu continues!

Starts.eu will continue providing calls, events, news and resources from S+T+ARTS and Science-Tech-ARTS projects. All the partners of S+T+ARTS will continue to share useful content on the field.

What will change for you?

The Community Space (Profiles, Map and Blog) will no longer be available. This means for you that you won´t have any more the possibility to register and create a profile in starts.eu, and you will not be able to change or access existing profiles. The content of the blog will also disappear. Eventually, the map will remain available for consultation as a legacy interface of the current STARTS community, but will no longer be updated.  

What will happen with your data (profiles and account details)?

The database will be archived by IRCAM – which hosts the current platform. IRCAM and all the partners of the STARTS Ecosystem project commit to protect your data from any potential commercial or other usage,  according to GDPR rules.

At any moment, you can send an email to donneespersonnelles (at) ircam (dot) fr and ask the suppression of your backup account.

Now if you want to continue following STARTS, the best is to subscribe to the newsletter.

Instagram | Facebook | Youtube.

We hope to see you again soon in S+T+ARTS events or projects!
